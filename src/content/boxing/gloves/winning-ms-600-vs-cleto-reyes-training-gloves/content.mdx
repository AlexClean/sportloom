<p className="article-p-mt4">People compare Winning and Cleto Reyes because both brands sit near the top of the boxing glove world. They are not cheap beginner gloves. They are premium training gloves that many serious boxers look at once they already know they want better gear.</p>

<p className="article-p-mt2">Cleto Reyes has a reputation for punch feedback. The gloves feel more traditional, more compact, and more connected when you land shots. That makes them exciting on the bag, but it can also make them less forgiving if your hands need extra protection.</p>

<p className="article-p-mt4">Padding is the biggest difference between Winning MS-600 and Cleto Reyes Training Gloves.</p>
<p className="article-p-mt2">Winning padding is soft, protective, and very good at reducing the sharp feeling of impact. This is why Winning is often described as one of the safest-feeling gloves for sparring and long training blocks.</p>
