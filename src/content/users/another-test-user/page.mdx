## Sizing & Ounces Guide

As a general rule, 10–12 oz gloves work well for bag and pads, while 14–16 oz gloves are more suitable for drills and sparring. Heavier athletes and dedicated sparring usually lean toward 16 oz for better protection.

## Amateur vs Pro: What You Need to Know

Amateur events often provide competition gloves, so your own gloves are mainly for training. Professional competition usually follows stricter rules on glove type, closure, and glove weight.
